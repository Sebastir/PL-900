 Concept
 AI Builder enables your business to use intelligence to automate processes and
 glance insights from your data in Power Apps and Power Automate.
You can build custom models tailored to your needs or choose a prebuilt model that is ready to use
for many common business scenarios.

Prebuilt -> Add intelligence without having to gather data and then build, train, pushing the Model
Custom --> Help you create more complex from the ground up

Models available for Business Scenarios
- Prediction model
- Business Card Reader
- Category Classification
- ID Reader
- Invoice Processing
- Key phrase extraction
- Language detection
- Receipt processing
- Sentiment analysis
- Text Recognition
- Text Translation
